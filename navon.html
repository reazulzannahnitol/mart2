Navon Jobs

Navon Jobs was created out of the desire to respond to the labor market needs in Romania, where there is an increasing demand for skilled workers. I proudly became the first and only recruitment agency in Romania with offices in the UAE, Nepal, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka. By collaborating with employers from various fields, we focus on recruiting candidates from Asia, particularly from these countries, bringing valuable skills and knowledge to Romania. An important aspect of our activity is to provide continuous support to both employers and employees, ensuring that their integration into the new work environment is seamless. We are dedicated to educating employers about the benefits of cultural diversity and providing them with the necessary tools to build efficient and united teams.

Navon Jobs is more than just a recruitment agency; it is my dream turned into reality, a platform dedicated to facilitating connections between employers and candidates from various corners of the world. Founded to meet the growing demand for skilled labor in Romania, I have succeeded in becoming the first and only recruitment agency in the country with offices in the UAE, Nepal, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka.

Vision and Mission

My vision for Navon Jobs is to promote cultural diversity and contribute to the integration of international labor into the Romanian economy. I believe that every person has the potential to add value, and our mission is to facilitate this process. We aim to create a working environment where diversity is celebrated and where all employees feel valued and supported.

Services Offered

International Recruitment: Recruiting talent from Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, and Nepal for various industries.
Assistance and Integration: Supporting both employers and employees through the integration process.
Education and Training: Educating employers on cultural diversity and providing tools for team building.

Impact on the Community

Navon Jobs not only supports employers in finding the best candidates but also contributes to community development by creating job opportunities. We take pride in having facilitated numerous job opportunities for people from various parts of the world who dream of a better life in Romania.

Continuous Improvement

In a constantly changing world, we are constantly adapting our strategies to respond to market needs. We are dedicated to the continuous improvement of our services and are always looking for innovative ways to make recruitment more efficient and accessible.
